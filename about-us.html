Who We Are

Ramay Textile (Pvt) Ltd is vertically integrated manufacturing unit for knitwear and hosiery garments. We have been making quality garment products for more than 30 years. We have build a huge network of clients all over the world by ensuring they trust our products to always be of the highest quality and delivered on time. We promise quality and timely delivery.

Vertical integration means that we can quality control products through out the process of knitting the garment, dyeing, cutting, printing/embroidery, stitching, right up till finishing and packaging. We have the complete range of machinery in-house to cater to various design specifications. If your designs require complex printing, wash or finishing processes, you could rest easy because we have the capability of taking care of all your needs at one place. In addition to that our location, in the centre of the textile manufacturing area of Lahore, enables us to get easy access to any process that we might not have in our factories.

Our History

  • 2013

    To focus on a marketing strategy which is beneficial to both us and our customers, we set up a marketing wing. Ramay Sourcing, aims to simplify the sourcing process for our customers by dealing with them directly and helping them perform the functions of a sourcing agent.

  • 2001

    To cater to the increasing demand for production capacity, we set up a fully vertical manufacturing unit, Ramay Textile (Pvt.) Ltd in Lahore, Pakistan. A much bigger and completed Knit to Pack setup meant significantly enhanced production capability and improved quality control.

  • 1986

    We started catering to the international market for hosiery garments with particular focus on Europe. Working directly with the customers, we established very strong ties with our clients and had ample growth in the demand of our products both locally and worldwide.

  • 1982

    Faisal Naveed Hosiery (FNH) was set up in Faisalabad, the textile capital of Pakistan. The newly set up manufacturing catered to the local demand for hosiery under garments. The brand recognition grew quickly and FNH expanded to meet the increasing demand.

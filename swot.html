SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Our Mission Statement, that is the driving force behind all our efforts. It goes like, “To break the threshold of all previous quality benchmark so as to satisfy our commitment to our work & also to our customers.”
  • Our Human Capital, the intellect and force behind this thriving organization that is responsible for painting such a promiseing picture for this organization.
  • Our Infrastructure, which gives us a unique stand among the biggest prduction houses of L:ahore. Giving our inteligent minds an energetic body to work on.
  • Our Supply Channels, that enable us to source the best for our ultimate customers i.e., our buyers & most important, the end users.
  • Close Communication, with our customers to give them the best-customized service ever possible.

Weaknesses

  • No Compromise on quality standards.
  • No Request for favors from our customers once we take things in our hands.
  • No Buisness without confirmed Letter of Credit.

Opportunities

  • As long as people prefer wearing clothes, we are looking forward to an open and growing market for our products.
  • Some of the growing markets like USA and Europe, where our customers respect our output and associations are promissed to thrive more in the coming days.
  • New markets worldwide where we are ready to enter, like never before.
  • Latest developments in the field of textile engineering, designing and fashion, Which is opening new ways for us to take the advantage of this ever expanding field.

Threats

  • Though the unforseen external forces i,e political and social, can not only effect the organizations but even the economies. That’s why we are trying to attain a position where our internal positive forces give us enough energy to out cast any unbalance in the external environment (if the situation ever arises).
